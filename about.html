About Us

A Great Place to Work. A Great Place to Receive Care. Leading Dentistry in Malabar.

At Dr. Mayan’s Aesthetic Dental Clinic, we combine modern technology with compassionate care to create healthy, confident smiles. Our clinic is designed to provide a calm, comfortable environment where patients feel valued and cared for at every step. From preventive care to advanced cosmetic treatments, we deliver excellence with precision and a gentle touch.

With a team of skilled professionals and state-of-the-art facilities, we ensure every patient receives personalized treatment tailored to their needs. At Malabar Dental, we don’t just focus on teeth — we focus on people, helping you achieve a lifetime of oral health and a smile that truly reflects your confidence.
